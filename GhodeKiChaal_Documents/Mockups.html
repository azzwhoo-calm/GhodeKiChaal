How to Play
The horse always moves in an L — two squares one way, one square across.
♞
Visit every square once Start anywhere. Hop square to square using only legal knight moves. Fill the whole board to win the tour.
🐎
Horses stay behind Every visited square keeps its own carved horse, and a dashed trail marks your route.
⟲
Stuck? Undo or restart If no legal move remains, the board stays visible so you can study it — undo a few hops or start fresh.
✦
Difficulty changes the help Apprentice shows the best move. Knight highlights legal moves. Master shows nothing at all.
